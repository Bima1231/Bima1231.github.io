About Me

I’ve always felt that life is more beautiful when I let myself go with the flow, without getting caught up in expectations or pressure. Every day, I wake up with a calm mind, enjoying the moment without rushing, and letting life take me to unexpected places. My motorcycle, always ready to accompany me, is a symbol of my freedom—there’s no fixed destination, just the enjoyment of the wind on my face and the hum of the engine as I ride down the road. I’m not obsessed with achievements or outcomes, because I know that happiness comes from the process itself, from the journey I’m on, not the destination I reach. By following the flow of life, I feel more alive, more free, and more grateful for every step I take.

In the midst of the classroom bustle, I often steal glances at you, trying to hide the feelings that keep growing inside me. Every smile of yours, the soft laughter I hear, and the way you speak make my heart race, yet I can't bring myself to say anything. I can only love you in silence, keeping my distance on the outside, even though inside I long to be closer to you, to share stories, or simply spend time together. But I know this feeling may only belong to me, and I choose to keep it deep inside, savoring our time as classmates, even though my heart continues to hope for something more.

My Projects

I am someone full of energy and a spirit of adventure, always seeking freedom on the road. With my Honda CB 100 as a loyal companion, every ride becomes more than just transportation—it's a way for me to express myself. My CB 100, with its retro design and reliable engine, carries nostalgic memories while remaining dependable on all types of terrain. Though it's no longer a young bike, it still offers outstanding performance, with light handling and a distinctive sound that adds a macho touch. Whether navigating the busy streets of the city or embarking on long-distance adventures, the CB 100 gives me the freedom I crave, making every ride a story of its own. It’s more than just a vehicle—it’s an inseparable part of my colorful journey through life.

The powerful 2-stroke engine and precise suspension give me confidence, whether navigating busy city streets or open tracks. Every ride with the Ninja SS is a new adventure that delivers an incredible thrill, and this bike is not just a vehicle—it's a part of my lifestyle, always pushing me to reach the peak in everything I do.

I am someone who values simplicity and has an unyielding spirit of adventure, and my Honda GL 100 is a loyal companion on every journey. With its light and responsive handling, as well as a smooth engine sound, the GL 100 gives me a true sense of freedom, making it more than just a vehicle—it's an integral part of my life’s journey, full of stories and adventure.
